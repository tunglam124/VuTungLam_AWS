# Sharing and Feedback

## General Assessment

### 1. Working Environment

The working and learning atmosphere at FCAJ left a very positive impression on me: friendly, close-knit, and not too rigid. The Admin team members were always enthusiastic in helping whenever I had questions, even willing to support outside office hours. The workspace was well-organized and clean, creating a comfortable feeling and helping me maintain focus throughout my work. If I could offer a suggestion, I think the company should organize more internal bonding activities, such as after-work social events, so that members — especially interns — have the opportunity to get to know each other and connect more deeply with everyone.

### 2. Support from Mentor and Intern Management Team

My mentor guided me with great dedication, always explaining thoroughly whenever I was confused and encouraging me to ask questions proactively. What I appreciated most was that my mentor often let me explore, experiment, and find my own way to solve problems before providing the answer — this approach helped me develop much more independent thinking. In addition, the intern management team provided thorough support with paperwork and administrative procedures, allowing me to focus on my professional work with peace of mind.

### 3. Relevance Between Real Work and Academic Major

Overall, the assigned tasks were closely aligned with the knowledge foundation I learned at university, while also opening up opportunities to approach new areas of knowledge and skills I had never encountered before. This combination helped me both consolidate my professional foundation and expand my practical understanding — something that a purely academic environment can hardly provide fully.

### 4. Opportunities for Learning and Personal Development

The internship period was an opportunity for me to acquire many practical skills: from using work/project management tools and teamwork coordination skills, to professional communication in a corporate environment. My mentor/admin also frequently shared valuable real-world experiences, helping me develop a clearer vision for my future career direction.

### 5. Company Culture and Team Spirit

The work culture here left me with a very positive impression: everyone respects each other, maintains a serious attitude toward work while still preserving a comfortable and pleasant atmosphere. Especially during tight project deadlines, the whole team worked together to support each other, regardless of hierarchy or role. Thanks to this, even as an intern, I felt that I was truly part of the team, not an outsider.

### 6. Internship Policies and Benefits

The company provides an allowance for interns and is quite flexible in arranging work schedules when needed. Moreover, the fact that interns are allowed to participate in internal training sessions is a notable plus, giving me additional learning opportunities beyond the scope of my direct assignments.

### 7. What I Was Most Satisfied With During the Internship

What I was most satisfied with was being trusted by my mentor/admin with tasks that had real meaning in the project, not just superficial work. Being directly involved in building the architecture diagram for the Cloud Nexus project helped me deeply understand how a serverless system on AWS operates in practice — an experience that would be difficult to visualize clearly through university theory alone.

### 8. What Could Be Improved for Future Interns

I believe the company could organize more systematic onboarding events and training sessions specifically for interns during the early phase, helping everyone become familiar more quickly with the common workflow and tools. Additionally, more internal bonding activities would also help interns integrate better with the company's culture.

### 9. Would You Recommend This Internship to Friends? Why?

Yes, I would recommend friends to intern here. The reason is that the working environment is quite open, mentors/admins are dedicated, and they always create conditions for interns to try real work and learn practical skills instead of merely doing simple, supportive tasks. This is a suitable environment for those who want to accumulate real-world experience rather than just stopping at theory.

## Suggestions & Expectations

### 1. Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?

I suggest that the company could build a clearer and more up-to-date onboarding roadmap for new interns, along with basic guidance documents on the processes and tools used in the project. This would help interns reduce the familiarization period and get started on real work more quickly.

### 2. Would you like to continue this program in the future?

Yes, I would like the opportunity to continue working with and contribute more to the company in the future, if conditions permit.

### 3. Other comments (free to share):

I would like to express my sincere gratitude to my mentor/admin and the entire team for providing the conditions and support throughout my internship. This was truly a valuable experience that helped me grow both in professional knowledge and practical working skills.
